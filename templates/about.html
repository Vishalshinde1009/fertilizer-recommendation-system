About The Project

AI plus agriculture for practical farm decisions

This platform combines soil chemistry, crop requirements, weather values, and machine learning to recommend suitable fertilizer.

Project Details

The Fertilizer Recommendation System is a full-stack Flask application that uses a Random Forest Classifier trained on crop, soil, and nutrient data. It stores prediction history in SQLite and provides PDF reports for field reference.

It is designed as a GitHub-ready, deployment-friendly project for students, farmers, agritech demos, and smart agriculture learning.

Benefits For Farmers

  • Improves fertilizer planning based on crop and soil conditions.
  • Reduces guesswork and supports data-driven nutrient decisions.
  • Provides clear usage guidance and soil improvement tips.
  • Tracks historical recommendations through a dashboard.
